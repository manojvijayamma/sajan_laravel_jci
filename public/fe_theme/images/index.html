BLASTLINE INDIA

Blastline India (P) Ltd is the pioneer and leader in the Anti-Corrosion Industry in India. It consists of a group of business entities catering to every need of the anti-corrosion industry in the country with overseas presence in various countries like Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrai,UK and USA through their Associate Companies.

Led by a dynamic management team, it has been able to adopt to changing needs of the industry and has forayed into all related niche areas like production and export of Machines, Accessories, Abrasives other specialized equipments and instruments used in the Anti-corrosion industry.
